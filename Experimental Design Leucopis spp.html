Experimental Design: Leucopis spp.

In this experiment, we are looking at the development of Leucopis spp. and their ability to reduce Hemlock Woolly Adelgid population numbers. Treatment 1 is a hemlock branch with no branch enclosure and no Leucopis spp. Treatment 2 has a branch enclosure but no Leucopis spp. Treatment 3 is a branch with an enclosure and 5M and 5F Leucopis spp.
